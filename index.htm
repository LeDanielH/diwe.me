I don’t avoid problems for the sake of the peace. I solve them, instantly if possible. I have learned by experience, that consequences of lying or not solving them are usually much worse. I have great relationships with those who understand this concept.

First comes an idea and then I learn whatever is necessary  in order to bring that idea to life. I’ve done that with my own projects and I intend to do the same thing with yours.
